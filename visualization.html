Some Analysis of Maharashtra Households Amenities Data

Histogram

Bellow histogram is an example of Taluka level good condition houses percentage of Scheduled Caste population. In the histogram, we can see a fraction of a variable of good condition houses percentage among the total percentage of good condition houses from the total percentage of census good condition houses. And the result is good we can see because most of the SC taluka have good condition houses.

Scatter Plots

Here is an example of scatter plots for Maharashtra Taluka-wise Scheduled Caste and General population ownership of own houses percentage for rural and urban basis. The scatter plots below show that SC and General both have similar kinds of friction for each taluka, but the main difference is between rural and urban. Rural people have more percentage of their own house ownership as compared to urban. Besides that, some taluka for both rural and urban general people have a higher percentage of their own house ownership, but SC people for that taluka have a low percentage of their own house ownership.

Here is an example of scatter plots for Maharashtra Taluka-wise Scheduled Tribe and General population ownership of own houses percentage for rural and urban basis. The scatter plots below show that ST and General both have similar kinds of friction for each taluka, but the main difference is between rural and urban. Rural people have more percentage of their own house ownership as compared to urban. Besides that, some taluka for both rural and urban general people have a very higher percentage of their own house ownership, but SC people for that taluka have a very low percentage of their own house ownership. For example, urban taluka general people have 78% own house ownership, and for that, urban taluka ST people have only 17% of own house ownership.

So among above from both SC and ST comparing with general, we can say that SC people have a higher percentage of own house ownership as compared to ST. Even ST taluka have more fluctuations than SC with general people percentage of own house ownership.
